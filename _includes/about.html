  Biography

I am 23 years old. I love watching movies and TV shows (not your every-day movie/tv show fan, I have an offline database of 2TB+ specifically for this) and listen to Rock/Pop/EDM music. I am also highly passionate about Graphic designing and I am an active follower of Quora. One F.R.I.E.N.D.S episode a day makes my day complete. I usually sleep very late at night. I watch european football and american football. I was brought up in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. I attended St Thomas Higher Secondary School and St Thomas Central School for my higher secondary education. My failures have motivated me more than my achievements. I have always kept myself open to learning newer technologies and above all, I am good at working under pressure with an upcoming deadline to meet thereby making myself open to new ideas till the very last mom

I did my Bachelor's degreee in Avionics Engineering at Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology. In my 3rd year in college I became passionate about Computer Science and subsequently Computer Networks. From then I took up electives related to Computer Science and diverted my area of studies from Avionics. I did my Internship and my Final year project on Software Defined Networking at IIST under B.S. Manoj . I came across open source projects such as OpenFlow and Open vSwitch during my Internship and Final year project. I am also fascinated by and interested in the working methods of the Opensource community. As a result, I strive to be a part of it some day. Previously, I worked as a Software Development Intern at Ather Energy - a smart electric vehicle start-up based out in Bangalore were I worked as a part of program automation and data intelligence teams. I also worked as a Research Associate in UpX Academy, startup ventured under Tech Mahindra. Here I concentrated on Data Science and Big Data technologies. Currently, I am a Graduate student in Computer Science with specialization in Data analytics (expected graduation in 2019) at Boston University. Currently I am working as a Data analytics intern at MetLife, RI for the summer of 2018. I also worked as a Quality Assurance Intern at BUworks (BU Information Technology & Services) from Fall 2017 to Spring 2018 and will continue my work during Fall 2018. My long-term goal is to expand my knowledge in Automation and Data-driven Technologies such as Cognitive Networks, Connected Vehicles etc incorporating my Interdisciplinary knowledge from Networks, Software Engineering, Data Science and my interest in Connected vehicles.
